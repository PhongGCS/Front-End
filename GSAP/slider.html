Adaptive Comfort in Real Time

Micro-adjusts temperature, airflow, and firmness as you move so you never wake up too hot or too cold; sensors learn your patterns nightly and tune the surface within seconds for a consistently balanced sleep climate.

Smooth Sleep Transitions

Guided vibrations ease you from light dozing into deep rest without jolting you awake, aligning with your breathing to reduce micro-arousals and restlessness so you drift naturally into deeper stages.

Gentle Wake Assistance

Slow-rise light and tone match your circadian rhythm so you wake clear, not groggy; vibration ramps softly to nudge you out of REM without spiking your heart rate or shocking you awake.

Breath-Synced Relaxation

Haptic cues mirror calm breathing to lower heart rate naturally and shorten sleep onset, with the bed humming in sync with your exhale to teach your body to downshift and quiet the mind.

Noise-Aware Quiet Mode

Adaptive soundmasking reacts instantly to spikes—traffic, voices, doors—before they wake you, filling only the gaps to keep the room serene so you sleep through chaos without heavy white noise walls.

Partner-Friendly Zoning

Independent sides personalize firmness, temperature, and cues without bleeding over, while motion isolation keeps your partner’s midnight shifts from reaching you so you can share a bed without sharing interruptions.

Sleep Insights You Can Use

Clear nightly summaries turn signals into simple, actionable tips you can actually follow—no dashboards to decipher, just one or two nudges to improve tonight and build better habits over time.
